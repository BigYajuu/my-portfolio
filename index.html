About this Website

This portfolio website founded by me showcases a catalogue of my creations and undergoing project(s) in their chronological order.

The website itself uses a framework that I have engineered using HTML, SCSS, TypeScript and jQuery. This means that most of the features, even the ones that seemed as simple as background transitions, has to be painstakingly done from scratch.

The high maneuverability of the web elements, traded by the prolonged development period for its initial release as a result, suddenly sparks even more ideas on future updates, and I hope that stays true for many years.
